How to use this calculator
  1. Enter the TOP measurement (weight)
  2. Enter the DOWN measurement (density)
  3. Enter the current BASE price
  4. Click "Calculate" to see results
  5. Save to Transactions for record keeping
Formulas Used

POUNDS = ROUNDDOWN(TOP/7.75, 2)

KARAT = ROUNDDOWN(TOP/DOWN, 2)

Moro = ROUNDDOWN((KARAT-10.51)*(52.838)/KARAT, 2)

VALUE = ROUNDDOWN((BASE*POUNDS*KARAT)/23, 0)
